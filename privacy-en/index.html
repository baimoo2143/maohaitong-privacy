Maohaitong - Privacy Policy

Welcome to Maohaitong! We highly value your privacy. Below is how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We only collect basic pet information you input (e.g., birthday, weight) and location data for nearby services.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as name, phone number, or address).

2. Data Usage

The data is used only to provide in-app features, such as calculating standard pet weight or displaying nearby pet hospitals and pet-friendly restaurants. This data is not stored on servers.

3. Data Protection

We will not sell, rent, or share your pet or personal information with any third parties.

4. User Rights

You can delete the app or stop using it at any time, and your data will no longer be accessed or used.
